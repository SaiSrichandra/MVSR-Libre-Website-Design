So in conclusion ...

Open source is a licensing agreement that allows user to freely modify a work, use said work in new ways, integrate the work into larger project or derive a new work based on original. By removing barriers between innovators, open source promotes a free exchange of ideas within a community to drive creative, scientific and technological advancements . MVSR Libre-Free and Open Source Developers Club Motivated the students to participate in Open Source projects and work on local community problems.
